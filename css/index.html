Community Bulletin

The city council announces a new weekend market pilot to support local artisans and small businesses. Residents can expect rotating vendors, food stalls, and music performances across three weekends. Volunteers are invited to assist with setup and visitor guidance. Sign up early to secure a preferred time slot.

A neighborhood tree-planting drive will launch next month to improve street shade and air quality. Families are encouraged to join, and each participant receives a starter kit with gloves, tools, and care instructions for saplings.

  • Market pilot runs every Saturday, 4 PM to 9 PM.
  • Volunteer check-in opens one hour before event start.
  • Tree-planting drive registration closes in two weeks.
